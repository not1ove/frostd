04 / THE EXPERIENCE NEW GEN PARLOR × BAR
NOT A BAR.
an experience.
FROSTD doesn't pour drinks. We don't run a bar. We're building a new-gen ice cream parlor × bar setup at AYCS 2026 — curating the full experience around the product, not just the product itself. No bottles. No pouring. No traditional bar mechanics. The activation is the experience.
THE COUNTER
Display freezer at the front. Visitors see the gelato. They order at the counter. Cups are scooped on the spot — no taps, no glassware, no bottles in sight.
THE WAFFLE STATION
Live waffle maker as part of the experience — pair with any drop. Adds smell, sound, and theatre to the activation. People stay longer.
THE FREEZER LINE
Backup freezer + chiller to hold full-day stock. Standard booth power requirements. Footprint and capacity flex to the setup AYCS gives us.
THE BRAND
Matte black cups. Foil-stamped marks. Birchwood spoons etched per drop. Kraft sleeves per event. The booth itself is content — photographed, posted, shared.
